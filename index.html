Marni Wild

These photo's were taken at the top of Wairere falls in Matamata. I was travelling the north Island in my camper-van (living the life). During my travels, I did a lot of hiking including the great walk Lake Waikaremoana.



About me

I was born in Palmerston North on the 23rd of september, as my mum was giving birth to me we both almost died as she was having severe blot clots. It was a miracle that we both survied the experience. After birth my parents moved, to Paekakariki where I grew up until the age of 5, when we moved to Wellington central.

While growing up in Wellington, I have always been an adventurous person, I've always enjoyed the outdoors and taking risks, when I was only 3 years old my parents lost me, and found me on top of a cafe roof, where I'd climbed up the drain pipe. From a young age I've also been quite musical and grew up with a wide taste in music. I started drumming since the age of 10, and was in a band at the age of 12.
